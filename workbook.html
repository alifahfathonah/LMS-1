Introduction Scenarios

You’re with your group of friends talking and the “new girl” walks by. Your friends immediately start talking about her because they think she is stuck-up. You can tell that she has heard them because she looks over at the group. You just had a class with her and thought she was pretty nice. The teacher paired you up with her to do an exercise, and she helped you complete it. You don’t want her to think you don’t like her, but she sees that you are a part of this group that is talking about her.

What do you do?
